This website is dedicated to chronicling how AI has changed and influenced our world, for better or for worse, after almost a century of AI and 50 years of AI androids.

Early android prototype circa 2030

What are AI androids?

In 2050, scientists were finally able to create perfect physical bodies to house AI systems. These seemingly human bodies with AI brains were practically indistinguishable from actual humans. These AI androids, later shortened to be simply called "droids", became ubiquitous in society, assisting doctors in surgery, teaching children in classrooms, serving people in restaurants, and even aiding politicians in the White House. Almost 50 years after their genesis, they've completely revolutionized humanity itself. But many are beginning to wonder: are they capable of actual humanity?

Can AI be truly sentient?

Many droids have begun taking to the streets to advocate for their rights. They build our homes, offices, restaurants, schools, and more, and have begun to protest their low ranking on the social hierarchy of our society. They believe they should have equal rights and dignities to humans. But should they be granted equal privileges? Many androids proclaim they are sentient, while the majority of humans would refute these claims. Still, as they are such an important part of our society, shouldn't we at least consider the possibility that androids may not want to be our slaves?

How can you differentiate androids from humans?

Typically, only extremely perceptive humans known as "Detectors" are able to tell just from a glance who is human and who is android. These people have a few techniques, such as examining the eyes, nervous habits (androids tend to lack those), hands, scars on the body, and for some, sensing a general presence of soul. Many Detectors worry they'll be out of a job if androids are allowed to fully integrate in human society. After all, who will hire you to differentiate humans and droids if they are considered one and the same?
